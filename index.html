1. Patentability Search

Assesses whether an invention is likely to meet novelty and inventive-step requirements before filing.

  • Targeted prior art search in patent and non-patent literature
  • Feature-wise comparison vs closest prior art
  • Clear opinion on strengths, gaps and drafting pointers
Patentability – Essential
For single inventions with clear claim structure.
  • Search across major patent databases
  • Top 5–10 key prior art references
  • Feature comparison table (invention vs prior art)
  • Short written opinion (2–3 pages)
Pricing: On request (per invention)
Patentability – Enhanced
For complex inventions or multiple independent claims.
  • Expanded search coverage (patent & non-patent literature)
  • Ranked prior art set with commentary
  • Detailed claim-chart style mapping
  • Recommendations for claim drafting strategy
Pricing: On request (per invention / per family)

2. Novelty Assessment

Quick, focused novelty check on specific claims or concepts.

  • Claim-focused searching around defined inventive features
  • Shortlist of closest documents with claim-feature mapping
  • Traffic-light style indication of novelty risk (green / amber / red)
Novelty Check – Rapid
For early-stage concepts and inventor discussions.
  • Single-concept search
  • 3–5 closest prior art references
  • One-page risk summary
Pricing: On request (per concept)
Novelty Check – Detailed
For near-final claims before filing.
  • Multiple claim variants covered
  • Feature mapping for each key claim
  • Structured summary for attorney review
Pricing: On request (per set of claims)

3. Infringement Risk Assessment

Evaluates whether a product or process is likely to fall within the scope of third-party rights.

  • Identification of relevant active patents in target markets
  • Claims vs product mapping with annotated charts
  • Risk categorisation and high-level design-around suggestions
Infringement Risk – Screen
For early FTO impressions before major investment.
  • High-level patent scan in key jurisdictions
  • List of potentially relevant families
  • Simple risk flags per family
Pricing: On request (per product / per market)
Infringement Risk – Detailed
For launches, licensing and due diligence.
  • Comprehensive patent search and filtering
  • Claims vs product charts for key patents
  • Consolidated risk summary and design-around notes
Pricing: On request (scope-based)

4. Invalidation Search

Identifies strong prior art to challenge a patent’s novelty and inventive step for litigation or post-grant proceedings.

  • Deep prior art search on independent and key dependent claims
  • Feature-wise mapping to identify novelty and inventive-step attacks
  • Structured evidence pack to support opposition or litigation teams
Invalidation – Core
For single target patent / key claim set.
  • Focused search around main independent claims
  • Shortlisted prior art with excerpts
  • Claim vs prior art mapping tables
Pricing: On request (per target patent)
Invalidation – Extended
For complex cases or multiple related patents.
  • Broader search across families and non-patent literature
  • Multiple attack lines (novelty or inventive step)
  • Evidence pack ready for litigation strategy
Pricing: On request (scope-based)

5. Patent Landscape Analysis

Strategic overview of patents in a technology domain, market, or competitor cluster.

  • Custom search strategy for the defined domain
  • Cleaning and clustering of relevant publications
  • Segmentation by assignee, geography, technology and timelines
Landscape – Snapshot
For quick strategic overviews.
  • Domain definition and search strategy
  • High-level stats and charts
  • Key assignees and technology clusters
Pricing: On request (per domain)
Landscape – In-Depth
For R&D and portfolio planning.
  • Deeper clustering and sub-domain analysis
  • Trends over time and white-space identification
  • Custom deliverables (slide deck / spreadsheet & report)
Pricing: On request (scope-based)

6. Patent–Product Mapping

Aligns product portfolios with relevant patents for risk, licensing and monetisation decisions.

  • Mapping of product features to internal and third-party patents
  • Identification of gaps, overlaps and white-space
  • Support for licensing, cross-licensing and portfolio optimisation
Mapping – Single Product
For key launches or flagship products.
  • Feature breakdown of the product
  • Mapping to relevant patents (internal & external)
  • Risk and opportunity summary
Pricing: On request (per product)
Mapping – Portfolio
For multiple products / platforms.
  • Portfolio-wide mapping matrix
  • Identification of coverage gaps and overlaps
  • Inputs for licensing and portfolio strategy
Pricing: On request (scope-based)
